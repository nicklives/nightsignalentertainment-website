About

The founding souls behind Night Signal Entertainment.

Nick Lives

Nick is an award-winning independent game developer of 11+ years, having served as the lead writer, artist and "marketing guy" on cult hits such as Doodle Date, We Need To Go Deeper, A Wonderful Day For Fishing and more. Nick’s work has been praised across his career as “imaginative”, “wildly funny” and “twisted” by outlets such as NPR, Kotaku and Bloody Disgusting, among others. Nick has also voyaged into other creative endeavors through the years such as writing for comedy websites and acting/puppeteering at terrible fantasy theme parks. In his free time, Nick enjoys watching the worst films ever put to celluloid and spending time with his cosplaying actress wife Kortney and their two pet gremlins.

David Johnsen

David Johnsen is a professional award-winning composer, sound designer, and business manager having worked on games such as We Need To Go Deeper, Night Signal, Camp Canyonwood and others over the past 11+ years. David graduated from BYU in 2020 with a Bachelor’s in Commercial Music and his work has since been featured by Sony, Microsoft, Nintendo and others. When he’s not composing tracks or filing taxes for Night Signal Entertainment, David enjoys speed-running through retro 90’s classics, hand-grinding delectable coffees and socializing with friends and family around a poker table.
